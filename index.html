About Us

Founded in 2009, rtCamp has been working exclusively on WordPress, with services
ranging from technology consultancy & full-fledged enterprise projects development to
optimizing the performance of e-commerce stores along with Nginx server setup and
management.

Other than services, rtCamp also has products like rtMedia – a plugin that allows users to
upload media on BuddyPress, and EasyEngine – a tool that makes WordPress on Nginx easy.

Apart from working on client projects and premium products, many rtCampers keep
contributing back to open-source projects with code, themes, plugins, participation at
meetups and WordCamps.
